Our Services

Abdict Resources Limited provides a wide range of financial and investment advisory services to companies, institutions, governments, and individuals. Our expertise spans capital raising, corporate finance, mergers & acquisitions, and strategic business transformation across Africa.

Financial Advisory

Strategic advice to enhance business performance and growth, including:

  • Corporate investment advisory for companies, institutions, and governments
  • Capital raising and debt/equity structuring
  • Mergers, acquisitions, and corporate finance transactions
  • Securities offerings and listings (private placements, rights issues, public offers)
  • Valuation, restructuring, and transaction advisory

Corporate Strategy & Business Transformation

Development and implementation of strategies to maximize efficiency and value:

  • Corporate restructuring and reorganizations
  • Divestitures, takeovers, and joint ventures
  • Business transformation and operational efficiency
  • Strategic planning for growth and market expansion

Corporate Governance Services

Governance, compliance, and regulatory advisory:

  • Compliance with SEC and Nigerian Exchange Limited regulations
  • Board advisory and governance frameworks
  • Regulatory compliance and reporting
  • Corporate secretarial services

Development Consulting Services

Advisory services for government, agencies, and institutions:

  • Capital market development advisory
  • Financial management consulting for public sector
  • Policy advisory and institutional capacity building
  • Public-private partnership (PPP) advisory

Due Diligence Reviews

Comprehensive reviews for investment and partnership decisions:

  • Transaction due diligence for mergers and acquisitions
  • Investment reviews and risk assessment
  • Compliance and regulatory due diligence
  • Financial and operational reviews

Feasibility & Business Planning

Project viability assessment and strategic planning:

  • Feasibility studies for new ventures and projects
  • Business plan development and review
  • Market entry strategy and analysis
  • Project viability and risk assessment

Investment Advisory & Research

Comprehensive investment advice tailored to client objectives:

  • Portfolio advice and asset allocation strategies
  • Economic, capital markets, and industry research
  • Investment opinions, reports, and recommendations
  • Securities analysis and investment strategy

Training & Capacity Development

Building financial expertise and market knowledge:

  • Training programs in investment and advisory
  • Financial literacy seminars and workshops
  • Capital market operations training
  • Capacity development for institutions and organizations

International & Local Partnerships

We collaborate with local and foreign firms, institutions, and agencies to provide comprehensive investment advisory services, subject to regulatory approvals where required. This enables us to bring global best practices and diverse expertise to our clients.

Strategic partnerships • Cross-border advisory • Joint ventures

Licensed & Regulated: Abdict Resources Limited operates in compliance with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and other regulatory bodies.
